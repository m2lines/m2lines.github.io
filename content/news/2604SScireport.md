The very first [Schmidt Sciences Impact Report](https://www.schmidtsciences.org/2025-report/) has been released, offering a snapshot of the impact made by its grantees and highlighting the importance of supporting foundational research.

Among the featured stories is [M²LInES](https://www.schmidtsciences.org/profile-2025/training-the-next-generation-of-climate-models/), recognized for its work on training the next generation of climate models and advancing AI-driven Earth system science.
