M²LInES: Multiscale Machine Learning In coupled Earth System Modeling

Welcome to M²LInES: a large international collaborative project for improving climate projections using scientific machine learning.

Our team includes scientists from New York University, Princeton, GFDL, Columbia, LDEO, NCAR, MIT, CNRS-IGE, and CNRS-IPSL.
